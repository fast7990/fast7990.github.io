    Yijing Mall

    Liaocheng Yijing Trading Co., Ltd. is a self operated e-commerce enterprise, dedicated to providing consumers with a pleasant online shopping experience. Since its establishment, it has adhered to the concept of "authentic goods" and zero tolerance for counterfeit goods.

    Through rich and user-friendly websites and mobile clients, Yijing mall provides a wide range of products and services with excellent quality at competitive prices, delivering them to consumers in a fast and reliable manner, and providing flexible and diverse payment methods.

    Yijing Mall is committed to creating a one-stop comprehensive shopping platform.

    Yijing cross-border e-commerce

    Liaocheng Yijing Trading Co., Ltd. has formulated detailed development plans for cross-border import and export business, comprehensively accelerating the internationalization process.

    In terms of import business, a "Global Purchase" platform has been established.

    In terms of export business, our multilingual global sales cross-border trade platform is committed to meeting the needs of global users, based on the global supply chain, and driving a massive number of "good Chinese goods" and "good Chinese merchants" to go global with a "globalization+localization" model. 

    Yijing technology

    Liaocheng Yijing Trading Co., Ltd. is a business and technology oriented company. Since its establishment, it has invested a large amount of resources to develop and improve its own technology platform that is reliable, capable of continuous upgrading, and centered on e-commerce application services.

    The value of technology can be summarized in two aspects: business assurance and technological breakthroughs. Business guarantee includes basic guarantee, platform guarantee, and security guarantee, which can support high-speed and smooth operation and cope with the massive order pressure of e-commerce promotion such as 618 and Double 11. On the basis of completing business guarantees, technological breakthroughs will bring higher operational efficiency and more business opportunities.

    A considerable proportion of current technological breakthroughs are generated from big data. For example, big data can help us improve user experience and operational efficiency, completing applications such as personalized recommendation search, automatic replenishment, and automatic pricing; At the same time, while completing the comprehensive business operation support, cloud will become the core of external technology and solution services, and will comprehensively cloud export its own technology, resources and experience to help the government and industry users quickly embark on the "Internet plus" process.

    Liaocheng Yijing Trading Co., Ltd. is committed to becoming a company that creates the greatest value for society, actively practicing corporate social responsibility in employment, taxation, globalization, and other aspects, and becoming a true national enterprise is our long-term goal.
